Features & Benefits

  • Search Donors: Easily find blood donors based on location, blood type, and availability.
  • Request Blood: Submit blood requests for specific blood types and quantities.
  • Emergency Alerts: Receive instant alerts during emergencies to mobilize donors quickly.
  • Donation History: Keep track of your blood donation history and contributions to the community.
  • Notifications: Stay informed about upcoming blood donation drives and events.

By using Blood Connect, users can save lives, contribute to their community, and make a positive impact on society.

How It Works

1 . Sign up for a Blood Connect account or log in if you're an existing user.

2. Search for blood donors in your area by specifying your location and required blood type.

3. Contact the donor directly or send a blood request through the app.

4. Once a donor is found, arrange for blood donation at a convenient location and time.

5. After the donation, both the donor and recipient can update their donation status on the app.
